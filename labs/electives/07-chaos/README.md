# Chaos Engineering

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2.5h  |  Primary tool: LitmusChaos  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Test resilience by injecting controlled failures.

## Your task

Install a chaos tool (LitmusChaos), run a pod-delete or network-latency experiment against a resilient Deployment, and confirm it recovers.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share the experiment result and evidence the app stayed available.
