# Hackathon Capstone

Track: Capstone  |  Estimated time: 6h  |  Primary tool: mixed  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Bring it all together in one deployed application.

## Your task

Design and deploy a multi-tier app end to end: Deployments, Services, config/secrets, persistent storage, health probes, an Ingress, resource limits, and a basic dashboard or monitoring. Document your architecture.

## Show-off checkpoint

Present your running app with a short architecture write-up and a live demo to your PLG.
