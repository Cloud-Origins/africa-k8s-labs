# Logging (EFK stack)

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2.5h  |  Primary tool: Elasticsearch/Fluentd/Kibana  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Aggregate pod logs centrally.

## Your task

Deploy an EFK (or Loki) stack, ship logs from your pods, and query them in Kibana/Grafana for a specific pod or error string.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share a Kibana/Grafana query surfacing logs from a specific pod.
