# Kafka on Kubernetes (Strimzi)

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2.5h  |  Primary tool: Strimzi  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Run a stateful data platform on Kubernetes.

## Your task

Install the Strimzi operator, create a Kafka cluster and a topic, then produce and consume messages from within the cluster.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share a producer sending and a consumer receiving messages on your Kafka topic.
