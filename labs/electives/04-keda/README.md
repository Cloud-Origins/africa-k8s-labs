# Event-driven Autoscaling (KEDA)

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2h  |  Primary tool: KEDA  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Scale on external events, not just CPU.

## Your task

Install KEDA and create a ScaledObject that scales a consumer on a queue/topic depth (or cron). Drive the event source and observe scaling.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share the ScaledObject and pods scaling with the event backlog.
