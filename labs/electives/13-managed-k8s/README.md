# Managed Kubernetes (AKS/EKS/GKE/LKE - pick one)

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 3h  |  Primary tool: cloud CLI  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Provision and use a managed cluster (cloud account required; not local).

## Your task

Create a managed cluster on one provider (AKS/EKS/GKE/LKE) with its CLI, get credentials, deploy a workload, and expose it with a cloud LoadBalancer.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share the external URL/IP of a workload running on your managed cluster.
