# Service Mesh (Istio)

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 3h  |  Primary tool: Istio  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Add traffic management and mTLS with a service mesh.

## Your task

Install Istio, enable sidecar injection on a namespace, deploy a sample app, and configure a VirtualService/DestinationRule to split traffic between two versions.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share a traffic-split demo (for example 80/20) and evidence of mTLS between services.
