# Disaster Recovery & Backup

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2.5h  |  Primary tool: Velero  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Back up and restore cluster state.

## Your task

Install Velero with a storage backend, back up a namespace, delete it, then restore from the backup and verify the workloads return.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share the delete-then-restore sequence with the namespace fully recovered.
