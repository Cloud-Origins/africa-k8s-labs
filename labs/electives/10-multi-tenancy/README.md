# Multi-Tenancy

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2h  |  Primary tool: kubectl  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Isolate multiple tenants on one cluster.

## Your task

Use namespaces, RBAC, ResourceQuotas and NetworkPolicies (or a tool like KubeZoo) to give two tenants isolated, capped, access-controlled space; prove tenant A cannot touch tenant B.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share a cross-tenant access attempt being denied.
