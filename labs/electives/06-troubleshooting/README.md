# Troubleshooting

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2.5h  |  Primary tool: kubectl  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Diagnose and fix broken workloads methodically.

## Your task

Work through failure scenarios (CrashLoopBackOff, ImagePullBackOff, failing probes, misconfigured Service selectors, pending pods) using describe, logs, events and exec; fix each.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share a before/after for one broken workload plus the root-cause you identified.
