# Platform Tooling (KubeSphere/Loft/Shipa/Portainer/DevSpace - pick one)

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2h  |  Primary tool: varies  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Explore a higher-level platform layer on top of Kubernetes.

## Your task

Pick one tool (KubeSphere, Loft/vcluster, Shipa, Portainer or DevSpace), install it, and complete its core workflow (for example a virtual cluster with Loft, or a dev loop with DevSpace).

## Show-off checkpoint

Share the tool's UI/CLI performing its core workflow on your cluster.
