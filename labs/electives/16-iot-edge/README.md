# Kubernetes for IoT/Edge

Track: Elective  |  Estimated time: 2h  |  Primary tool: kubectl  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Run Kubernetes at the edge with lightweight distributions.

## Your task

Use a lightweight distro (k3s/MicroK8s) to model an edge deployment, deploy a small workload, and discuss resource constraints and disconnected operation.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share your edge workload running on a lightweight cluster and its resource footprint.
