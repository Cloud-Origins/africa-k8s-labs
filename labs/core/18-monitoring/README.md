# Monitoring (Prometheus & Grafana)

Track: KCNA, CKAD  |  Estimated time: 2.5h  |  Primary tool: Prometheus/Grafana  |  Type: Lab

Grading: manual. There is no autograder for this lab; your mentor reviews your show-off checkpoint in your PLG space.

## Objective

Collect and visualize cluster metrics with the Prometheus + Grafana stack.

## Your task

Install kube-prometheus-stack with Helm into a monitoring namespace, port-forward Grafana, log in, and open a cluster/pod resource dashboard. Explore how Prometheus scrapes targets.

## Show-off checkpoint

Share a Grafana screenshot of a dashboard showing live CPU/memory for your workloads.
